Our Delicious Desserts

Chocolate Cake

To make a Chocolate Cake, mix 1 cup flour, ½ cup cocoa powder, 1 cup sugar, baking powder, and a pinch of salt. Add milk, oil, and vanilla essence, then blend into a smooth batter. Pour into a greased pan and bake at 180°C for 30–35 minutes. Cool, frost with chocolate icing, and serve.

Waffles

To make Waffles, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, milk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla essence into a smooth batter. Pour it into a preheated waffle maker and cook until golden and crisp. Serve warm with honey, chocolate syrup, or fruits.

Hot Chocolate

To make Hot Chocolate, heat milk in a pan and add cocoa powder, sugar, and a little chopped chocolate. Stir until smooth and creamy. Pour into a cup, top with whipped cream or marshmallows, and serve hot.

Fudge Brownies

To make Fudge Brownies, melt butter and chocolate together, then mix in sugar, eggs, and vanilla essence. Add flour and cocoa powder, stir well, and pour into a greased baking tray. Bake at 180°C for 25–30 minutes. Cool, cut into squares, and serve.
