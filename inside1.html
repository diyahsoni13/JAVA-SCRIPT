Our Delicious Sandwiches

Club Sandwich

To make a club sandwich, toast three bread slices and spread mayonnaise on each. Layer one slice with lettuce, tomato, and cucumber; place another slice on top with cheese and grilled chicken or paneer. Add the third slice, press gently, and cut diagonally. Serve with ketchup or fries.

Grilled Cheese Sandwich

To make a grilled cheese sandwich, butter two bread slices on one side each. Place cheese between the unbuttered sides, then grill or toast on a pan until both sides are golden and the cheese melts. Serve hot with ketchup or soup.

Tomato Cucumber Sandwich

To make a tomato cucumber sandwich, spread butter or mayonnaise on two bread slices. Layer thinly sliced tomato and cucumber, sprinkle a little salt and pepper, then cover with the other slice. Cut in half and serve fresh.

Veg Mayo Sandwich

To make a veg mayo sandwich, mix chopped vegetables like carrot, cucumber, and capsicum with mayonnaise, salt, and pepper. Spread the mixture on bread slices, cover with another slice, and cut in half. Serve chilled or toasted.
